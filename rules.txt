1. The player plays against the dealer
2. The dealer plays their first card face up and their second card face down.
3. The goal of the game is to get closer to 21 than the dealer WITHOUT going over 21.
4. King, Queen, Jack is 10. Ace can be 11 or 1.
5. If the player beats the dealer, they win the pot. If they get 21, they win double.
